Branding Idea

What does the company do or offer?

The company is a cafe that offers refreshments while providing a relaxing social space for its potential customers. The menu features a variety of refreshments like coffee, tea and teas. On the company site, it will show its company location, contact information, items that are on their menu and an online ordering system that allows customers to pay directly on the site.

Who is the company to potential customers?

As the company is a cafe, it offers potential customers a place to sit and relax, a place to study and do work, or a place to spend time with friends and family. With the website, the company hopes to give potential customers an easy way to view the cafe's menu and order online. Clicking into the menu and ordering the drinks will be straightforward so it can cater to a wider range of ages for people to use.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

With a simple and minimalistic style website layout, those who visit the website can easily navigate around. Moreover, the website can provide details of what the drinks are made with and how much it is. Along with the option to order drinks online on the website, it gives potential customers an opportunity to skip the lineup for on-the-go pickups or just order at their table directly for those who wants to stay a longer period of time.

Brand Components

Fonts

Main font - Quicksand Bold 700
Sub font - Quicksand Semibold 600
Sub font - Quicksand Regular 400

The font chosen is a sans-serif font that has a simple and round look to it. With using a font weight, it helps to create and keep a minimalistic style to the website with it still being legible on a screen. The main font with the font weight bold 700 will be used for headings like h1, h2 and h3. The first sub font with the font weight semibold 600 will be used for h4 headings. Lastly, the sub font with the font weight regular 400 will be used for p.

Colour Palette

Main colour - #d9cebe
Sub colour - #ac9585
Accent colour - #ffffff
Accent colour - #b2c8b0

The colour palette is chosen to reflect the colours of a matcha expresso latte. The main colour is used for the web page background colour. The sub colour is used as the main font colour and link navigation button background colour. The white accent colour is used as the background for sections and also the font colour of link navigation buttons. Lastly, the green accent colour is used for hovers. The green accent colour adds a pop to the existing soft warm brown tones and helps to highlight and give emphasis to the existing elements on the web page.
